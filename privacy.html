Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  •  KS Movies OTT Platform

Your privacy matters to us. KS Movies is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use KS Movies, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you register
  • Usage Data: Movies watched, search history, and preferences
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store card numbers)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device model, and operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our streaming service
  • To personalize your movie recommendations
  • To process subscription payments
  • To send important service notifications
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase — an industry-standard, encrypted cloud database. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

KS Movies uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database
  • YouTube: Video content delivery (subject to YouTube's Privacy Policy)
  • PhonePe/GPay/Paytm: Payment processing

5. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. You can clear these through your browser settings, but this may affect your experience.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Update your account information

7. Children's Privacy

KS Movies is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or a notice on our platform.
